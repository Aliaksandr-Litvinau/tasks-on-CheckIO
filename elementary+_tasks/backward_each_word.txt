In a given string you should reverse every word, but the words should stay in their places.

Input: A string.

Output: A string.

Example:

backward_string_by_word('') == ''
backward_string_by_word('world') == 'dlrow'
backward_string_by_word('hello world') == 'olleh dlrow'
backward_string_by_word('hello   world') == 'olleh   dlrow'
